Part I 01

The F.S.P.T.E. System

Five pillars build your writing brain — from word to sentence to paragraph to types to full essays. Master them in order, and any writing becomes possible.

خمس ركائز تبني عقلكِ الكتابي — من الكلمة، إلى الجملة، إلى الفقرة، إلى أنواع الفقرات، إلى المقالة الكاملة بكل أنواعها. اتقنيها بالترتيب، وكل ما تكتبينه يصبح ممكنًا 🌿

F oundation

Grammar essentials. Verb tenses, articles, prepositions, the verb be.

أساسيات القواعد. الأزمنة، الأدوات، حروف الجر، الفعل be.

S entence

Subject + verb. Statements & questions. Capitals & punctuation.

الفاعل والفعل. الجمل الخبرية والاستفهام. الترقيم والأحرف الكبيرة.

P aragraph

Format, topic sentence, supporting details, conclusion. Unity & coherence.

الشكل، الجملة الموضوعية، التفاصيل الداعمة، الخاتمة. الوحدة والترابط.

T ypes

Descriptive, example, process, narrative, opinion paragraphs.

الوصفية، المثالية، الإجرائية، السردية، والآراء.

E ssay

Full essays — 7 types. Hook, thesis, body paragraphs, clincher.

المقالة الكاملة — 7 أنواع. الجملة الافتتاحية، الأطروحة، الفقرات، الخاتمة.

Pillar I F

Foundation — Grammar for Writing

The grammar you need to write a clear sentence. Click any card to open the lesson with examples, Arabic explanations, and a mini quiz.

القواعد التي تحتاجينَها لكتابة جملة واضحة. اضغطي على أي بطاقة لفتح الدرس مع الأمثلة والشرح العربي واختبار قصير.

F.1 a
Verb Tenses
الأزمنة (مضارع، ماضي، مستقبل)

When does the action happen? Present, past, future — the foundation of every sentence.

F.2 b
Verb Be — Am/Is/Are
الفعل Be

The most-used verb in English. Connect subjects with adjectives, places, and identities.

F.3 c
Subject–Verb Agreement
اتفاق الفعل مع الفاعل

Singular subject = singular verb. Plural subject = plural verb. The classic trap.

F.4 d
Articles — A, An, The
أدوات التعريف والنكرة

When to say a, when to say an, when to say the, and when to say nothing.

F.5 e
Prepositions of Place
حروف الجر للمكان

In, on, under, between, behind. The little words that locate everything in space.

F.6 f
Adjectives
الصفات

Words that describe nouns. The key to descriptive writing.

Pillar II S

The Sentence — Your building block

Every paragraph is built from sentences. Master the subject–verb pattern, statements, questions, and the punctuation that finishes them off.

كل فقرة مبنية من جمل. اتقني نمط الفاعل والفعل، والجمل الخبرية، والاستفهامية، وعلامات الترقيم التي تنهيها.

S.1 a
Subject + Verb
الفاعل والفعل

A sentence = a complete idea = a subject + a verb. The simplest, most important rule.

S.2 b
Statements & Questions
الجمل الخبرية والاستفهامية

Same words, different order. How to flip a statement into a question.

S.3 c
Negation
النفي

Saying no. Not, don't, doesn't, am not, aren't.

S.4 d
Capital Letters & Punctuation
الأحرف الكبيرة والترقيم

Start with a capital. End with a period, question mark, or exclamation.

S.5 e
Fragments & Run-ons
الجمل الناقصة والمتراكبة

The two most common sentence mistakes — and how to spot and fix them.

S.6 f
Conjunctions
أدوات الربط

And, but, so, because, or. The glue that joins ideas together.

Pillar III P

The Paragraph — A complete idea

A group of sentences with one main idea. Learn the format, the three-part structure, and the secrets of unity and coherence.

مجموعة من الجمل لها فكرة رئيسية واحدة. تعلّمي الشكل، والبنية الثلاثية، وأسرار الوحدة والترابط.

P.1 a
Paragraph Format
شكل الفقرة

Title, indent, margins, spacing. The visual rules every paragraph follows.

P.2 b
The Topic Sentence
الجملة الموضوعية

The first sentence. Topic + controlling idea. Tells the reader what to expect.

P.3 c
Supporting Sentences
الجمل الداعمة

The middle sentences. They explain, prove, or develop the topic sentence.

P.4 d
The Concluding Sentence
الجملة الختامية

The last sentence. It wraps up the paragraph and leaves the reader with a thought.

P.5 e
Unity — One main idea
الوحدة

Every supporting sentence must connect to the topic sentence. No off-topic ideas.

P.6 f
Coherence — Logical order
الترابط

Ideas in a clear order: by time, by space, or by importance.

Pillar IV T

Paragraph Types — Five ways to write

Different goals need different paragraphs. Describe a person, give examples, explain a process, tell a story, or share an opinion.

أهداف مختلفة تحتاج فقرات مختلفة. صفي شخصًا، أعطي أمثلة، اشرحي خطوات، احكي قصة، أو شاركي رأيك.

T.1 a
Descriptive Paragraph
الفقرة الوصفية

Describe a person, place, or thing. Use specific adjectives and the verb be.

T.2 b
Example Paragraph
فقرة الأمثلة

Support a topic with specific examples. Uses the simple present tense.

T.3 c
Process Paragraph
الفقرة الإجرائية

Explain how to do something, step by step. Uses time-order words and imperatives.

T.4 d
Narrative Paragraph
الفقرة السردية

Tell a story in order. Uses simple past and past continuous.

T.5 e
Opinion Paragraph
فقرة الرأي

Share your opinion with reasons. Uses there is/are and because.

Pillar V E

The Essay — 7 types to master

A paragraph is one idea. An essay is a full argument — introduction with a hook, multiple body paragraphs, and a strong conclusion. Master these 7 essay types and you can write anything.

الفقرة فكرة واحدة. المقالة حجة كاملة — مقدمة بجملة افتتاحية، فقرات متعددة، وخاتمة قوية. اتقني هذه الأنواع السبعة وستستطيعين كتابة أي شيء.

Start here ★
From Paragraph to Essay
من الفقرة إلى المقالة

How a paragraph grows into an essay. The 3-part structure: Intro → Body → Conclusion.

E.1 a
Descriptive Essay
المقال الوصفي

Paint a vivid picture using all 5 senses, similes, and metaphors. Sample: The Mango.

E.2 b
Narrative Essay
المقال السردي

Tell a true story with rising action, climax, falling action, and a lesson learned.

E.3 c
Cause & Effect Essay
مقال السبب والنتيجة

Show what causes what. Explain the effects of social media, diets, or any phenomenon.

E.4 d
Argumentative Essay
المقال الجدلي

Take a position. Acknowledge the other side. Defend your opinion with reasons.

E.5 e
Analysis Essay
المقال التحليلي

Analyze an idea or behavior. Give 2+ reasons with examples and explanations.

E.6 f
Compare & Contrast Essay
المقارنة والتباين

Show similarities and differences. Two subjects, side by side.

E.7 g
Problem-Solution Essay
مقال المشكلة والحل

Identify a real problem. Propose realistic solutions. Block or point-by-point.

Models M

Writing Models — Learn from examples

Read three real "introduce yourself" paragraphs from real students. See how they organize their ideas — then write your own.

اقرئي ثلاث فقرات حقيقية لطلاب يُعرّفون بأنفسهم. شاهدي كيف ينظّمون أفكارهم — ثم اكتبي فقرتكِ الخاصة.

Model 1 a
Shaukat from Pakistan
نموذج: شوكت من باكستان

A short, clear introduction. Name, country, language, family, future plans.

Model 2 b
Marta from Colombia
نموذج: مارتا من كولومبيا

A student introduction with full name, age, where she lives, hobbies, and goals.

Model 3 c
Calvin from China
نموذج: كالفن من الصين

A richer introduction explaining a name, age, school, work, and future business goals.

fourth pillar

Speaking 🗣️

Speak before you're ready. The mouth needs reps just like a muscle — perfect is the enemy of fluent.

01

Daily speaking drills

  • Talk to the mirror for 3 minutes about your day
  • Record yourself, listen back without flinching
  • Describe what's around you out loud
  • Shadow one minute of a podcast, daily
  • Argue both sides of a tiny opinion
02

Conversation partners

  • italki — affordable tutors
  • Cambly — instant convos
  • Tandem — language exchange
  • Voice notes with a study buddy on WhatsApp
03

Speaking prompts

Describe the last meal you cooked — every step, every smell.
If you could time-travel to one year, which and why?
What is something most people get wrong about your country?
Teach me something you learned this week, like I'm a child.
04

Common sounds to drill

  • th — thin / this / weather
  • v vs w — vest / west
  • i vs ee — ship / sheep
  • silent letters — knight, doubt, comb
  • schwa /ə/ — the laziest, most common vowel
